Vista High Secondary School

Vista High Secondary School is a public School located within the City of Cape Town District Military Rd,
Schotsche Kloof. The school falls in Cape Town Metropolitan Municipality, METRO CENTRAL and is a Q5.

The purpose of the school is to advance the the basic education the applicants already carry. Teaching, showing and
sharing them techniques to counter act future unexpects. The school's sub-objective is to help stimulate
problem-solving skills, and doing so the school issues a number of study subjects which are classified to 2 different
and are known as science & buiness.
Availbale subjects: Business, Physics, Life Science, Geography, Mathematics, IsiXhosa HL and English HL. As such,
Vista High is part of an education intervention that aims to redress the Apartheid education system.
